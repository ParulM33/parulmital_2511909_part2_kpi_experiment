# Hypothesis Test Notes – Paid Conversion Rate

 

## Objective

 

The objective of this test is to check whether the new onboarding experience (Treatment group) improves paid conversion rate compared to the Control group.

 

---

 

## Hypothesis

 

- Null hypothesis (H0): Treatment conversion rate is less than or equal to Control conversion rate 

- Alternative hypothesis (H1): Treatment conversion rate is greater than Control conversion rate 

 

---

 

## Test Details

 

- Test type: One-tailed two-proportion z-test 

- Significance level: 0.05 

 

---

 

## Data Used

 

- Control group: 22 conversions out of 690 users 

- Treatment group: 50 conversions out of 710 users 

 

---

 

## Results

 

- Control conversion rate: 3.19% 

- Treatment conversion rate: 7.04% 

- Absolute increase: 3.85 percentage points 

- Relative increase: 120.87% 

 

- Z-statistic: 3.26 

- P-value: 0.000549 

 

---

 

## Decision

 

Since the p-value is less than 0.05, the null hypothesis is rejected.

 

---

 

## Conclusion

 

The Treatment group shows a statistically significant improvement in paid conversion rate compared to the Control group.

 

This means the new onboarding experience is effective in improving conversion.
